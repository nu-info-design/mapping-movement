This project illustrates the evolving landscape of internal displacement in Ukraine over the past three years of war, focusing on oblast-level arrivals of Internally Displaced Persons (IDPs). It also explores how measurement methods have advanced and at times, been challenged.

The data source is the International Organization for Migration’s Global Data Institute, which has conducted 40 rounds of Area Baseline Assessments to track internal displacement across Ukraine. For more details, visit the Data Biography or the IOM’s website.
